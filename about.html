About Me

Get Know About Me!

Welcome! I’m Sarina Vatandoustan, a clinical psychologist dedicated to helping individuals and families navigate their mental health journey with compassion, evidence-based practices, and a personalized approach.


Background and Qualifications:

I hold a Master’s degree in Clinical Psychology and have spent years honing my skills in both private practice and clinical environments like Shafa Hospital. My expertise spans a range of therapeutic approaches including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Schema Therapy, and Neurotherapy. Through my experience, I have supported clients of all ages, from children to adults, in overcoming a wide spectrum of challenges.


My Approach & Philosophy:

My approach to therapy is integrative and tailored to each client’s unique needs. I combine evidence-based practices such as CBT, which focuses on changing unhelpful thought patterns, with Schema Therapy, which targets deeply rooted emotional patterns. Additionally, I incorporate Neurotherapy to help clients regulate brain function and improve emotional well-being. Whether working individually or in groups, I strive to create a safe, empathetic space where clients feel heard and supported. I specialize in treating a range of mental health issues including depression, anxiety, OCD, trauma, PTSD, and dyslexia. I’m also trained in marriage and sex therapy, helping couples navigate complex relational dynamics. My therapeutic expertise also extends to supporting clients through addiction recovery (MMT), as well as guiding children and teens with learning and behavioral challenges. I believe that every individual has the potential to lead a fulfilling life, no matter the challenges they face. My goal is to empower clients by helping them gain insight into their behaviors and emotions, develop healthier coping mechanisms, and ultimately build a life aligned with their values and goals. I am committed to creating a non-judgmental, supportive environment where healing and growth can flourish.
